XRate — Live Currency Conversion on Any Webpage

Instantly see prices converted to your preferred currency, right on the page. No more switching tabs to a converter — XRate detects prices automatically and shows the converted amount inline.

FEATURES
• Automatic detection of 40+ currencies including Middle Eastern currencies by symbol ($, €, د.إ, ر.س, …) and code (USD, EUR, AED, SAR, …)
• Live exchange rates supporting 160+ currencies including Gulf region (AED, SAR, QAR, KWD, BHD, OMR, JOD)
• Converted prices appear inline, right next to the original — no popups, no overlays
• Works on any website including shopping sites, travel portals, and news
• Handles US (1,234.56), European (1.234,56), and plain number formats
• Dark mode support
• One-click toggle to enable/disable
• Zero tracking, zero ads — your browsing stays private

HOW IT WORKS
1. Install XRate
2. Click the extension icon and select your target currency
3. Browse any website — prices are automatically converted inline

SUPPORTED CURRENCIES
USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, CHF, CAD, AUD, NZD, CNY, INR, HKD, SGD, KRW, THB, MYR, IDR, PHP, SEK, NOK, DKK, PLN, CZK, HUF, RON, BGN, ISK, TRY, BRL, MXN, ZAR, ILS

MIDDLE EASTERN CURRENCIES (NEW!)
AED (UAE Dirham), SAR (Saudi Riyal), QAR (Qatari Riyal), KWD (Kuwaiti Dinar), BHD (Bahraini Dinar), OMR (Omani Rial), JOD (Jordanian Dinar), EGP (Egyptian Pound), IQD (Iraqi Dinar), LBP (Lebanese Pound)

Supports Arabic currency symbols: د.إ, ر.س, ر.ق, د.ك, د.ب, ر.ع, د.ع

PRIVACY
XRate only connects to open.er-api.com (ExchangeRate-API) to fetch exchange rates. It does not collect, store, or transmit any browsing data. All processing happens locally in your browser. Zero tracking, zero ads.
