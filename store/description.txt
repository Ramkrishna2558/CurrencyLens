CurrencyLens — Live Currency Conversion on Any Webpage

Instantly see prices converted to your preferred currency, right on the page. No more switching tabs to a converter — CurrencyLens detects prices automatically and shows the converted amount inline.

FEATURES
• Automatic detection of 30+ currencies by symbol ($, €, £, ¥, ₹, …) and code (USD, EUR, GBP, …)
• Live exchange rates from the European Central Bank (ECB)
• Converted prices appear inline, right next to the original — no popups, no overlays
• Works on any website including shopping sites, travel portals, and news
• Handles US (1,234.56), European (1.234,56), and plain number formats
• Dark mode support
• One-click toggle to enable/disable
• Zero tracking, zero ads — your browsing stays private

HOW IT WORKS
1. Install CurrencyLens
2. Click the extension icon and select your target currency
3. Browse any website — prices are automatically converted inline

SUPPORTED CURRENCIES
USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, INR, CAD, AUD, CHF, CNY, SEK, NZD, KRW, SGD, HKD, NOK, MXN, BRL, DKK, PLN, THB, IDR, HUF, CZK, ILS, PHP, TRY, ZAR, MYR, RON, BGN, ISK

PRIVACY
CurrencyLens only connects to frankfurter.app to fetch exchange rates. It does not collect, store, or transmit any browsing data. All processing happens locally in your browser.
